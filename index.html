What is the AI

Today, more and more people understand that neural networks are the future, and that they can do things that were previously impossible.

Like any innovative product, neural networks seem to a wide audience as something of little use, but interesting.

They know how to write music, process and generate images, highlight the main points, voice text, and maintain a simple dialogue.
But after the first delight, everyone will play enough, and the new product will become commonplace in all areas.

How to use AI

The world has created neural networks that can draw pictures in any existing artistic style, confidently beat the world champion in the most complex logic game on the planet, record music albums and imitate human behavior in electronic correspondence. All of the above is so far only a demonstration of some of the capabilities of the technology, the real application of which both in business and in everyday life, we will see in the near future.

In other words, neural networks will make it possible not only and not so much to replace human labor in more complex work activities, but to become a useful tool for specialists and managers in many fields.

AI Security

Specialists from the Kryptonit company conducted a large-scale study of the safety of artificial neural networks, which are widely used in computer vision systems, speech recognition and in-depth analysis of various data, including financial and medical. The company announced this on November 29, 2022.

Experts compared the attacks on machine learning (ML) models built on the basis of artificial neural networks (ANN) described in scientific articles, reproduced different implementations of attacks and talked about the 10 most obvious ones.
